About:

Developed October 2016 as a basic showcase of the Shine On You Crazy API. Dedicated to my hermano Joseph Banuelos, who showed me Floyd was so much more than dad music.

I developed this as a project for the MEAN Stack Intensive through V School. As you may notice, the API database is not complete, and currently only displays US-based data for the major releases. My hope is to continue to flesh out the API towards completion, and to offer it as a resource for those learning (Such as the PokeAPI and Star Wars API were for me).
